### Sevenoaks Circular

A relatively quite route with traffic only on Poll Hill and the A21.  A good series of modest climbs and speedy descents.

Starting along the A21 and up though Knockholt to the top of Pol Hill.  A speedy decent down Pol Hill, turning left down to Otford.

Then a quick clime up to Kemsing before dropping down to Seal.

A steady climb up through Gothurst Common and short climb into Ide Hill Village.  Followed by a long, fast decent to Sundridge.

A fairly flat ride before the climb up Poll Hill, with a fast decent back through Knockholt and along the A21.

[Sevenoaks Circular](https://ridewithgps.com/routes/50763224){target=_blank .md-button}
