# Cycling Kit

Start your kit with padded cycling shorts and a nice fitting jersey.

The more cycling you do the more you should build up a collection of essentials.


<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   __Essential kit__

    ---

    - Bib shorts
    - Base layer
    - Jersey
    - Jacket
    - Shoes & Cleats
    - Gloves
    - Socks
    - Glasses

-   __Cold & Wet Weather__

    ---

    - Winter base layer
    - Mid-layer fleece
    - Winter gloves
    - Knee & leg warmers
    - Arm warmers
    - Gilet
    - Over-socks
    - Over-shoes
    - Snood (neck warmer)

</div>
